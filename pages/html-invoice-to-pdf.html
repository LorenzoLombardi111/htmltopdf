Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert an HTML invoice to PDF?

Open freehtmltopdf.app, paste your HTML invoice code or click Templates and select Invoice, preview the rendered invoice, choose A4 or Letter page size, then click Download PDF.

Does the invoice template include line items and totals?

Yes. The built-in invoice template includes company header, billing address, itemized line items with quantity and rate, subtotal, tax calculation, and total amount.

Can I customize the invoice styling?

Yes. Edit the CSS in the HTML to change fonts, colors, layout, and branding. The live preview shows changes in real time so you can fine-tune the design before generating the PDF.

Is the invoice data stored anywhere?

No. freehtmltopdf.app processes everything in your browser. Your invoice data, including client details and amounts, never leaves your device.

What page size is best for invoices?

A4 is the standard for most countries. Letter (8.5 x 11 inches) is standard in the US and Canada. Both work well with the built-in invoice template.

Can I add my company logo to the invoice PDF?

Yes. Include your logo as a base64-encoded image in the HTML using an img tag with a data URI. External image URLs may not load due to browser security restrictions.
